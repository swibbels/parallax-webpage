About

Have you ever visited a webpage that appears to shift as you scroll? That is known as the parallax scrolling effect. Parallax scrolling is a visual effect that makes the background of a webpage appears to be stationary or move at a slower pace than the foreground of the page. Doing so produces an optical illusion of depth, which adds to the user's experience.
